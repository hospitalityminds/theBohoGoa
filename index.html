Stay and Enjoy

Located in Vagator, in close proximity to the Ozran beach, Boho Goa is one of the best luxury hotels in Vagator and is your perfect destination to unwind and experience Goa hospitality! The rooms in their embrace are a picture of elegance and tranquillity. Walk the length of the rooms with private balconies, 4-star accommodations with an indoor pool, a sun terrace, an in-house restaurant, bar and feast your eyes on the beautiful garden of the hotel. Boho Goa located near ozran beach adds old-world charm to your Goa holiday, and the hotel is in walking distance to famous restaurants, clubs, flea markets, airport and beaches.
